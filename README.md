## Project Overview

This project analyzes Blinkit's retail sales data using Power BI to uncover business insights related to sales performance, outlet characteristics, product categories, and customer ratings.

The dashboard provides an interactive view of key business metrics and helps stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Evaluate outlet performance by size, type, and location.
- Identify sales trends across years.
- Compare product categories and fat content.
- Monitor customer ratings and inventory distribution.

---

- Power Query
- DAX
- Excel (Data Source)

## Key Insights

### Sales Performance
- Total sales exceeded $1.20M.
- Average sales per item reached $141.

### Outlet Analysis
- Tier 3 outlets generated the highest sales.
- Medium-sized outlets contributed the largest share of revenue.

### Trend Analysis
- Peak sales occurred around 2018.
- Sales stabilized after a temporary decline.

### Product Analysis
- Customer ratings remained consistent across product categories.
- Low Fat and Regular products showed similar customer satisfaction levels.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Expand operations in Tier 3 locations.
2. Invest more in medium-sized outlets.
3. Investigate drivers of peak sales performance.
4. Improve customer satisfaction to achieve ratings above 4.0.
5. Optimize underperforming outlet types.
